# FreshAF Launch Campaign — ready to post

*Everything below is copy-paste ready. Swap `freshaf.netlify.app` for `freshaf.io` when the domain is live.*

## Positioning (one line, use everywhere)
**FreshAF — on-demand car wash & laundry. Vetted pros come to you. Consider it done.**

## Handles & bios (grab these when creating accounts)
- Handles to try: `@freshaf.io`, `@freshaf_za`, `@getfreshaf`
- **Instagram/TikTok bio:** `Your car washed. Your laundry done. Wherever you are. 📍 Joburg — wait, no emojis? Socials allow them; brand voice: minimal.`
  Use: `Car wash & laundry, on demand. Vetted pros come to you. Johannesburg. Consider it done.`
- **Facebook about:** `FreshAF connects you with vetted mobile car wash and laundry professionals. Book in seconds, see the exact price upfront, pay card or cash, earn money back on every order.`

## Content pillars (rotate these three, never post outside them)
1. **Effortless** — your time back; the service happens while you live your life.
2. **Trust** — vetted pros, live tracking, upfront pricing, money-back rewards.
3. **Hustle** — pro recruitment; earn on your schedule, keep 85%.

## Launch sequence

### Week −1 — Teaser (3 posts)
1. Brand card (S4 image): caption →
   `Something clean is coming to Joburg. Consider it done. #FreshAF #Joburg`
2. Shine close-up (S3): →
   `Your car. Your laundry. Handled where you stand. Launching soon. #FreshAF #OnDemand #Johannesburg`
3. Story T1 with countdown sticker.

### Week 0 — Launch (post daily)
1. **Launch post** (S1): →

   `Every order pays you back. Earn 5% of every completed order as rewards and spend it on your next one. The wash that funds the next wash. #FreshAF #Rewards`

   `Wash cars? Do laundry? Your phone should be ringing. Join FreshAF as a pro — set your own area and hours, keep 85% of every job, get paid for skills you already have. Apply in minutes → freshaf.netlify.app (Become a pro) #JobsZA #SideHustle #Joburg`

   `From booking to done in one tap. Watch. Then try it yourself → freshaf.netlify.app #FreshAF`

### Ongoing (2–3× per week)
- Before/after washes (ask pros to shoot 10s clips — best organic content you will ever get)
- Pro spotlights: `Meet Sipho. 47 washes. 4.9 stars.` (trust pillar)
- Rewards balance screenshots from real customers (with permission)
- Suburb launches: `Now washing in Rosebank.`
