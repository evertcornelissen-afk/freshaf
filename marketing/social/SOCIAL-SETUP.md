## Instagram (business account)

1. instagram.com/accounts/emailsignup/ (or the app)
2. Fields:
   - **Full name:** `FreshAF`
   - **Username:** `freshaf` → if taken: `freshaf.io` → if taken: `freshaf_za`
3. After signup: Settings → Account type → **Switch to professional → Business** → category **Local service**.
4. Edit profile:
   - **Profile photo:** `avatar.png` (in this folder)
   - **Bio:**
     `Car wash & laundry, on demand.`
     `Vetted pros come to you. South Africa.`
     `Consider it done.`
   - **Website:** `https://freshaf.io` (add once the domain is live)
5. Post the 3 teasers (below) once the domain is live — not before.
